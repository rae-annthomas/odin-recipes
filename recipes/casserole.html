Casserole

Ingredients

4 cups dried mafalda pasta (mini lasagna noodles) 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 carrots, sliced 2 stalks celery, sliced 1 onion, chopped 1 zucchini, quartered lengthwise and sliced 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 (15-ounce) can red kidney beans 1 (15-ounce) can cannellini beans 1 (15-ounce) can diced tomatoes with Italian herbs 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce 1 cup frozen cut green beans 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning 1/2 teaspoon salt 1/3 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese chopped fresh basil and/or oregano.
